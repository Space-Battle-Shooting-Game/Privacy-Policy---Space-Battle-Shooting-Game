Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23-Nov-2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the SPACE BATTLE – SHOOTING GAME mobile application collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information Collected

a. Information Provided by Users (Optional)

If a user contacts support, the following information may be collected:

  • Name
  • Email address

No financial information is collected.

b. Automatically Collected Information

The app may collect the following information automatically:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • IP address (country-level only)
  • Crash reports
  • Performance and usage data
  • Analytics data (session time, gameplay patterns)

c. In-Game Data

The following gameplay information may be collected:

  • Levels completed
  • In-game progress
  • Coins earned through gameplay and rewarded video ads

2. How Information Is Used

  • Improve game features and performance
  • Provide in-game rewards (coins) for watching ads
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze gameplay activity
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

3. Sharing of Information

Information may be shared only with:

  • Advertising partners that provide rewarded video ads
  • Analytics and crash-reporting services
  • Legal authorities if required

No personal information is sold.

4. Data Security

Data is stored securely using encryption (HTTPS), secure servers, and limited access controls. Although no method is completely secure, reasonable steps are taken to safeguard user information.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 18 and older. Personal data from children under 18 is not knowingly collected.

6. User Rights

Users may request:

  • Access to their data
  • Correction of stored information
  • Deletion of their account and data

7. Data Retention

Data is retained only for app functionality, analytics, security, and legal compliance if required. When a user requests deletion, applicable data is removed promptly.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Changes will appear on this page with a new Last Updated date.
